HISTORIA MAMBOLEO ART AND CULTURAL SUMMIT 2022

The National Museums of Kenya aims to preserve, protect and promote Kenya's rich heritage and presents it through History, Culture, Nature and Contemporary Art

2022.24.05 24(FRI) ~ 26(SUN)

Main Program

Lecture

Learn about current exhibitions, conservation projects, the Museum's rich collection, and other topics through talks by leading experts.

Memory Lane

Tour the preserved colonial farmhouse, a kitchen in a separate building, a coffee-drying plant in the woodland, and an agricultural college that showcases plants.

Story time

Explore the historical transformation and hear about interesting tales of our ancestry and explore the different ways in which our predecessors lived

Culture

Museums display much more than artifacts; Museum Culture makes us on a tour through the complex of ideas, values and symbols in society that need to be represented.

Socialize

The Mamboleo Historia Global Summit brings together more than forty international speakers to share action orientated presentations on culture.
