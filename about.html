HISTORIA MAMBOLEO ART AND CULTURAL SUMMIT 2022

The Mamboleo Historia Summit brings together cultural and history enthusiasts from all over the world to come together and celebrate their culture. It was founded in 2012 when the museum went through a rebranding. It was decided that yearly, there would be summits where local and international guests come together to celebrate culture.
